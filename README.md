### 💡 The Vision

I'm organizing a **community AI mini-lab** where students in Abomey-Calavi, Benin will collaborate to build AI prototypes addressing real local challenges:

- 🌱 **Agriculture**: Plant disease identification using computer vision
- ♻️ **Environment**: Waste sorting through image recognition  
- 🚌 **Transportation**: Data-driven demand prediction models

**Why this matters:** AI shouldn't be a distant technology. Students in Abomey-Calavi can—and should—create solutions for their own community.
